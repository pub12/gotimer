Can I customize my interval lengths?

Yes! While the classic Pomodoro is 25 minutes, you can use our duration chips or customize settings in your profile to fit your specific workflow.

How do Challenges work?

Challenges pair you with friends or strangers. You both start timers; whoever stays in the "Focus Zone" (active browser window) longest wins the duel.
